Indegredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Topping of choice (lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until just combined, but don't overmix.
  2. Divide the mixture into four equal portions and form into 3/4-inch thick patties.
  3. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until they're cooked to your liking.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty if you want.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a separate pan.
  7. Assemble the hamburgers by placing a patty on the bottom bun and adding your favorite toppings.
