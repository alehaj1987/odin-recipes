Lasagna Recipe

Indegredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the ground beef over medium-high heat. Drain off any excess fat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and let it cook for at least 30 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. To assemble, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Arrange a single layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  8. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles. Top with 1/3 of the remaining meat sauce and 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat the layers: noodles, the rest of the ricotta mixture, 1/3 of the meat sauce, and 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese.
  10. For the final layer, top with the last of the noodles, the remaining meat sauce, and the rest of the mozzarella cheese.
  11. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until it's bubbly and the top is golden brown.
  13. Let the lasagna stand for 10 minutes before serving.
